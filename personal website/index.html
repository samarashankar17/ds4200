    Hi! My name is Samara Shankar and I'm a second year studying data science and economics with a minor in Law and Public Policy. I like to go outside to hike as I really enjoy the great outdoors! I also like to spend time with my friends and my girlfriend, doing things like watching tv or getting boba. I really enjoy doing arts and crafts for fun, and participate in studio art club and mural club with one of my roommates.

    I recently got into running. I was not a big runner in high school even though I was an athlete (I played on the tennis team), and since coming to college have basically not exercised at all besides being on an intramural pickleball team with my friend Charlie. I always thought that I was terrible at running because I wasn't very fast, but my girlfriend had me run a mile with her the other day, and I realized that it's actually not that hard to run a mile, because you don't need to always go fast! We ran about a 10 minute mile, which is obviously bad, but I wasn't that tired and realized I do have some stamina in running. This inspired me to get sneakers and start running, and my current goal is to do a 10K by January.
